\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
\subsubsection*{template$<$typename T$>$\newline
class helib\+::\+Const\+Cube\+Slice$<$ T $>$}

A constant lower-\/dimension slice of a hypercube. 

A \mbox{\hyperlink{classhelib_1_1_const_cube_slice}{Const\+Cube\+Slice}} acts like a pointer to a lower dimensional constant subcube of a hypercube. It is initialized using a reference to a hypercube, which must remain alive during the lifetime of the slice, to prevent dangling pointers. The subclass \mbox{\hyperlink{classhelib_1_1_cube_slice}{Cube\+Slice}} works also with non-\/constant cubes and subcubes.

In addition, for greater flexibility, a \char`\"{}slice\char`\"{} may be initialized with a vector and a signature, rather than a cube
