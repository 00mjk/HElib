Generate a key-\/switching matrix and store it in the public key. The i\textquotesingle{}th column of the matrix encrypts from\+Key$\ast$\+B1$\ast$\+B2$\ast$...$\ast$B\{i-\/1\}$\ast$Q under to\+Key, relative to the largest modulus (i.\+e., all primes) and plaintext space p. Q is the product of special primes, and the Bi\textquotesingle{}s are the products of primes in the i\textquotesingle{}th digit.
